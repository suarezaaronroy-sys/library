Sari2POS

Browser POS · sari-sari operations

A point-of-sale built for how Filipino corner stores actually run: the ones on notebooks, mental math, and a good memory for who owes what. Fast product grid, an utang ledger with real per-customer balances, cash / GCash / utang tender, multi-cashier PIN login with roles, restock and low-stock alerts, and end-of-day batchout with drawer reconciliation. It runs entirely in the browser and nothing leaves the device.

Offer design · operations spine

Politician Tier List

Civic record · parked concept

A tier-list built on the public record: politicians ranked and rated against what they actually did, not what they said. Sourced, cited, and structured so the reasoning travels. Barely a concept yet; the shape and scope are still being figured out.

Not started: parked here so it nags me.
