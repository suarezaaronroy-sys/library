In development · playable

Sari2POS

A point-of-sale built for how Filipino corner stores actually run — the ones on notebooks, mental math, and a good memory for who owes what. Fast product grid, an utang (store-credit) ledger with real per-customer balances, cash / GCash / utang tender, multi-cashier PIN login with roles, restock and low-stock alerts, end-of-day batchout with drawer reconciliation. It runs entirely in the browser and nothing leaves the device.

Trades CRM — design & build

Not a template — a done-for-you CRM and operations build for plumbers, electricians, glaziers, and construction firms. The whole lead-to-cash spine: quoting, scheduling and dispatch, invoicing, deposits and utang, and the automations that run it — on GoHighLevel, with the reasoning documented instead of trapped in one person's head. It's the engine behind Grimoire 001, offered as a service.
