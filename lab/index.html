Sari2POS

Point-of-sale for the sari-sari store

A local-first POS built for how Filipino corner stores actually run — fast product grid, an utang (store-credit) ledger with per-customer balances, cash/GCash/utang tender, multi-cashier PIN login with roles, restock and low-stock alerts, end-of-day batchout with drawer reconciliation, and receipts. Runs entirely in the browser; nothing leaves the device.
