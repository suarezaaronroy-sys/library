340 cold contacts, Liverpool landlords, 34.2% average open rate, 6.8% CTR, 18 consultations booked. The single biggest lever wasn't design, send time, or list hygiene — it was the subject line carrying the city. "Liverpool landlords:" beat the generic equivalent by 11 percentage points, consistently, across the sequence.

The lesson generalizes: specificity is the cheapest trust signal in cold email. A reader filters "for landlords" as spam and "for Liverpool landlords" as possibly-about-me.
