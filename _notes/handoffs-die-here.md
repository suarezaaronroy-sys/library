Watch where projects actually stall and it's rarely inside anyone's lane — it's at the seams. The work passes; the context doesn't. The receiving person inherits a file, a ticket, a half-thread, and none of the reasoning that produced it, so they start over, guess wrong, or wait.

A handoff is complete when the receiver can act without asking a question. That's the standard. Everything else — status, history, the *why* behind the current state — travels with the work or the handoff didn't happen.

This pattern scales straight into AI operations: agents inherit the same seams, minus the human improvisation that papers over them. The orchestration version of the argument is in [The Orchestration Layer]({{ '/grimoires/010-orchestration-layer.html' | relative_url }}).
