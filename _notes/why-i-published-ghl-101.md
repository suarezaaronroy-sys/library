When a new GHL user has to pay $200 to learn how workflow triggers interact with contact tags, that's not a content opportunity — it's a structural failure. The knowledge is scattered; the paywalls concentrated it; the people who need it most can least afford it.

So I published the manual: every procedure as exact click-paths, every claim cited to an official source, free, no gate. The bet is simple — open documentation builds more trust than gated documentation builds revenue. [Grimoire 003]({{ '/grimoires/003-ghl-101.html' | relative_url }}) is the result.
