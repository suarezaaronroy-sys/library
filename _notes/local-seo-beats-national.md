A new rental business will never outrank Rightmove for "flats to rent UK" — those terms are owned, permanently, by sites with millions of pages and decades of authority. Competing there is a budget bonfire.

The realistic path is the long tail the giants can't be bothered with: "landlord letting agent [specific neighborhood]," street-level content, local landmarks, borough-specific compliance questions. Low volume per term, but the intent is razor-sharp and the competition is often literally nobody. Stack fifty of those and you have a channel.
