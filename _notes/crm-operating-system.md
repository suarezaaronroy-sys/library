A filing cabinet stores what happened. An operating system decides what happens next. The difference in a CRM is whether the data drives behavior: does a stage change trigger the follow-up, assign the owner, start the clock — or does it just sit there waiting for someone to remember?

When the CRM is the operating system, the pipeline is the process, the fields are the vocabulary, and the automations are the policy. The 76-field build in [Grimoire 001]({{ '/grimoires/001-asmc-crm-engine.html' | relative_url }}) is what that looks like in production.
