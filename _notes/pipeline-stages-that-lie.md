Ask three reps what "Proposal Sent" means and you'll get three answers: drafted, emailed, or discussed-on-a-call-and-promised. The forecast built on that stage is fiction with a percentage attached.

Stages lie when they describe intentions instead of events. The fix is definitional, not technical: every stage gets one entry condition — observable, binary, written down. "Proposal Sent" means the document left the building, timestamped. If a stage can't be defined that way, it isn't a stage; it's a feeling, and feelings don't forecast.
